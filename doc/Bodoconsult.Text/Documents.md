Generating, viewing, exporting and printing structured documents with LDML
===============================

LDML is a markup language to define the content of structured text documents with paragraphs, headings, images, figures etc.. With the help of renderers the LGML markup can be translated into file formats like HTML, TXT and PDF.

## Block elements

### General document structure

-   **Document**: The root tag of a document

-   **Section**: A section of a document. A document must have at least one section

### Content of the sections

-   **Paragraph**: A paragraph with text

-   **Heading1**: A heading on level 1

-   **Heading2**: A heading on level 2

-   **Heading3**: A heading on level 3

-   **Heading4**: A heading on level 4

-   **Heading5**: A heading on level 5


-   **Paragraph**: A paragraph with text

## Inline elements

-   **Span**: Simple text

-   **Bold**: Bold text

-   **Italic**: Simple text

-   **Image**: A simple image without counting

-   **Figure**: A simple image with counting

-   **Equation**: A simple image with counting
